Plastic waste facts & figs

20.000.000 kg of waste is spilt into the North Sea each year, most of which is single use plastic. Plastic does not break down biologically but breaks up into ever smaller pieces and accumulates in our marine environment. These small pieces of plastic have infiltrated every level of our food chain and are by now finding micro plastics in supermarket fish and our dinner plates. Plastic production has increased 20 fold within the last 50 years. If we continue the way we do there will be more plastic than fish in our oceans by 2050.
