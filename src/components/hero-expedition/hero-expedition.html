The Plastic Soup Surfer

Merijn Tinga, better known as The Plastic Soup Surfer, is on a mission. He wants to stop the increasing plastic pollution of the marine environment. His campaigns are aimed at preventing plastic litter in the first place. The surfboard he uses for this journey is entirely made from plastic litter and is a statement by itself. Merijn will paddle down the Rhine in 28 days. In #Source2Sea, he will show how much plastic litter is found along the Rhine and demonstrate that this litter eventually washes out to sea and is lost forever.

He will focus on the sources of riverine plastic litter and will hold accountable the companies that produced the plastic products. He calls out to you to help address the chief executives of the plastic producing and packaging companies by sending them a formal notice. Because we need a change!
