The Plastic Soup Surfer

Merijn Tinga, better known as The Plastic Soup Surfer, is on a mission. He wants to stop the increasing plastic pollution of the marine environment. His campaigns are aimed at preventing plastic litter. The surfboard he used for this expedition are made from plastic litter and is a statement by itself.

Merijn Tinga is a trained biologist, has a career as a visual artist and in now a full time plastic activist.
